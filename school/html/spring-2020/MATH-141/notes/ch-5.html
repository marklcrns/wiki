Chapter 5: Trigonometric Functions

5.1 Ratio Nirvana and Inverse Functions

  • Handout 13: Ratios nirvana v1
  • Handout 14: Inverses intro
  • Handout 15: Ratios nirvana v2
  • Handout 16: Soh-Cah-Toa formula guide

In order to solve the triangle, at least one side and either a ratio or an angle are needed.

SOH-CAH-TOA

  • Functions that gives the ratios of 2 sides of a right triangle when the angle is known.
    • \(\color{#ff0000}{S}in(\theta) = \frac{\color{#ff0000}{O}pp}{\color{#ff0000}{H}yp}\), aka sine.
    • \(\color{#ff0000}{C}os(\theta) = \frac{\color{#ff0000}{A}dj}{\color{#ff0000}{H}yp}\), aka cosine.
    • \(\color{#ff0000}{T}an(\theta) = \frac{\color{#ff0000}{O}pp}{\color{#ff0000}{A}dj}\), aka tangent.

Inverse functions

  • Functions that gives the angles of a right triangle when the ratio is known.
    • \(sin^{-1}(\frac{Opp}{Hyp}) = \theta\), aka arcsine.
    • \(cos^{-1}(\frac{Adj}{Hyp}) = \theta\), aka arccosine.
    • \(tan^{-1}(\frac{Opp}{Adj}) = \theta\), aka arctangent.

5.3 The Reference Triangle and Rest of Functions

  • Handout 15: Ratios nirvana v2
    • Reference Triangles: page 53
    • The Rest of the Trig: page 96
  • Reference angles are drawn starting from positive \(\mathit{X}\) axis.
  • Positive angles are drawn counter-clockwise from positive \(\mathit{X}\) axis.
  • Negative angles are drawn clockwise from positive \(\mathit{X}\) axis.

Cosecant, Secant and Cotangent

  • \(sin(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{opp}}{\text{hyp}}\), \(csc(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{hyp}}{\text{opp}}\)
  • \(cos(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{adj}}{\text{hyp}}\), \(sec(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{hyp}}{\text{adj}}\)
  • \(tan(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{opp}}{\text{adj}}\), \(cot(\theta^{\circ}) = \frac{\text{adj}}{\text{opp}}\)
