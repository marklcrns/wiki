# Section 6.5: Length of Curves

## Definition: Arc Length for $y = f(x)$

Let $f$ have a continuous first derivative on the interval [a, b]. The length of
the curve from (a, f(a)) to (b, f(b)) is

$$
L = \int_{a}^{b} \sqrt{1 + f^{\prime}(x)^{2}}dx
$$
